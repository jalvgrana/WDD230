Target Audience

Two Personas:

Julia

Julia is a native from Coruña (Spain). She is a mother of 5 and works from home as a computer designer. She has little time to do house’s chores and needs a reliable source to know if she has to hang or not her clothing to dry outside. Having a large family means having to use the washer-machine during the day and although she has a drier, she loves having clothes outside in her back-yard. But weather changes very much in Coruña and at home there is always much debate and different opinions on how the weather will be like in a few hours. She needs to use her time in an efficient manner and wants to plan things first thing in the morning.

Roberto

Roberto lives in Oviedo (Spain) is a company-seller and is visiting his clients all day long. That means having to spend pretty much all his working-day from one place to the other. When visiting clients in the down-town he cannot use his car an must walk to his appointments. Deciding whether to take his umbrella or not is a big deal: it is not the first time he believed the weather would be fine but started to rain heavily and arrived to the place totally soaked. Or believed that it was going to rain and arrived to the meeting with an umbrella and clients started to make jokes about him to discover later on that because it was sunny he forgot his umbrella in the meeting room having to go back and lose time in between appointments. He would like to know exactly if he has to take or not his umbrella with him.

Scenario 1

Rich wants to go to the mountain for a walk and he doesn’t know if taking his boots or lighter shoes that are more comfortable but not waterproof.

Scenario 2

John is going to Church early in the morning for a meeting. He wants to know if short-sleeve or not is going to be fine.

Scenario 3

Richmond Family is going from St. George to Logan to visit Grandpas but they don’t know if it is going too cold up there. They want to know what clothes to wear.

Scenario 4

Philip has decided to start running Saturday’s early morning and wants to know if he must take hat and gloves and a sweater or just a t-shirt will be more than fine.

Scenario 5

Agnes is a senior-woman, retired living in Montana. She doesn’t want to get a cold. That always makes her feel terrible and takes a month to recover. She would like to know if the weather is going to change during the day or not.
